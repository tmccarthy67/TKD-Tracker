ONE-STEP SPARRING

1st STEP
-While moving right foot out, block with right inside block.
-Punch with left fist to abdominal area.
-Punch with right fist to abdominal area.
-Punch with left fist to face area (Ki-hap).

2nd STEP
-While moving left foot out, block with left inside block.
-Punch with right fist to left ribcage area.
-Punch with left fist to left ribcage area.
-Punch with right fist to face area (Ki-hap).

3rd STEP
-While moving right foot out, grab arm/wrist with left hand.
-Strike the right bicep of the attacker with your right forearm.
-Attack with right elbow to ribcage.
-Attack with right backfist to face area.
-Punch abdomen with right fist, uppercut chin with right fist, then right elbow to chin area (Ki-hap).

4th STEP
-Move left foot out and do a cross hand block.
-Grab the right wrist of the attacker.
-Place your left hand on the attacker’s right tricep, twist/circular motion, and pull the attacker down towards the ground.
-Bring your right knee up towards the attacker’s chest/face (Ki-hap).

5th Step
-Lightly block with your left arm and hook the attacker’s arm.
-Trap attacker’s right hand with your left arm and body.
-Move right leg in circular motion (180 degrees) at same time of trapping the arm.
-Do a right elbow strike to attacker’s kidneys.
-Do a right elbow strike to attacker’s head (Ki-hap).

6th STEP
-Do a right circular scoop block to attacker’s right punch.
-Do a left punch to right ribcage.
-Step outside the attacker’s legs and do a right ridge hand to attacker’s chest.
-Turn 180 degrees and do a left knife hand to attacker’s chest.
-Grab the attacker’s left shoulder with your right hand and do a right ankle sweep.
-Do a left punch to face (Ki-hap).

7th STEP
Attacker—right front kick and right punch.

Defender—left low block swings to left scoop/outside block.  Follow with right punch to attacker’s chest (Ki-hap).

8th STEP
Attacker—right front kick, right punch, then left punch.

Defender—left low block followed by right inside block to right hammerfist/outside block.  Follow with left middle punch to attacker’s chest (Ki-hap).

9th STEP
Attacker—right front kick, right punch, left punch, right upper block, and left punch.

Defender—left low block, right inside block to right hammerfist/outside block, left punch, right upper block, and left punch (Ki-hap).

10th STEP
Attacker—right roundhouse kick, left punch, right punch, left scoop block, right scoop block, and left punch.

Defender—left outer block, left inside block, left outer block, right punch, left punch, after attacker scoops your left arm then swing your left arm down and around to block attacker’s left arm to side, and right punch (Ki-hap).

11th STEP
Attacker—right roundhouse kick, left punch, right punch, left scoop block, right scoop block, left punch, bring left elbow backwards to knock defender’s arm out of the way, and right punch.

Defender—left outside block, left inside block, left outside block, right punch, left punch, swing left arm in circular motion to block attacker’s left arm to side, right punch to ribcage; after attacker knocks your right arm away then grab the attacker’s right arm, right stamp kick to back of attacker’s right knee, and right elbow to attacker’s head (Ki-hap).

12th STEP
Attacker—right roundhouse kick, left punch, and right punch.

Defender—left outer block, push left punch away with right hand, push right punch away with left hand, step through with right elbow to attacker’s right kidney, left elbow to ribcage, and left backfist (Ki-hap).

13th STEP
Attacker—right front kick, right punch, left punch, right punch, slide right leg backwards and bend forward at the waist.

Defender—X block, right inside block to right outside block, left hand blocks attacker’s right punch to the left, do a right palm heel strike to attacker’s right collarbone, right foot sweeps attacker’s right foot, and bring right elbow down on attacker’s neck (Ki-hap).

14th STEP
Attacker—right front kick, right punch, left punch, right punch, bend down on right knee.

Defender—X block, right inside block to right outside block, left hand blocks attacker’s right punch to the defender’s right after stepping out to the left, right roundhouse kick (ball of the foot) to midsection…without setting your foot down after roundhouse kick…right side kick to back of the attacker’s right knee, right elbow or knife-hand strike to attacker’s neck (Ki-hap).

15 STEP SPARRING (Do 3 times on each side)
Attacker—step forward right foot to fighting stance.
          -left arm upper block
          -right arm middle punch
          -right arm circle to block knife hand
          -right arm middle punch
Defender—step back left foot to fighting stance.
          -right knife hand to head
          -left hand pushing block
          -right hand open palm checking block
Attacker—Step forward left foot to fighting stance.
          -right arm upper block
          -left arm middle punch
          -left arm circle to block knife hand
          -left arm middle punch
Defender—step back right root to fighting stance.
          -left knife hand to head
          -right hand pushing block
          -left hand open palm checking block
Attacker—left arm outer forearm block.
          -right arm middle punch
Defender—right knife hand to throat.
          -right open hand (cup) down block
Attacker—right arm outer forearm block.
          -left arm middle punch
Defender—left knife hand to throat.
          -left open hand (cup) down block
Attacker—left arm outer forearm block.
          -right arm middle punch
Defender—right knife hand to throat.
          -right open hand (cup) down block
