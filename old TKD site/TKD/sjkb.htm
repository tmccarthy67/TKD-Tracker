SA JU KONG BONGS

    1. Pivot to left/down block, step forward reverse punch, pivot to left/down block, step forward reverse punch, pivot to left/down block, step forward punch, pivot to left/down block, step forward punch (Ki-hap).
    2. Pivot to left/upper block, step forward reverse upper punch, pivot to left/upper block, step forward upper punch, pivot to left/upper block, step forward upper punch, pivot to left/upper block, step forward upper punch (Ki-hap).
    3. Pivot to left/outer middle block, step forward middle punch, pivot to left/outer middle block, step forward middle punch, pivot to left/outer middle block, step forward middle punch, pivot to left/outer middle block, step forward middle punch (Ki-hap).
    4. Pivot to left/knife-hand block, step forward upper punch, pivot to left/knife-hand block, step forward upper punch, pivot to left/knife-hand block, step forward upper punch, pivot to left/knife-hand block, step forward upper punch (Ki-hap).
