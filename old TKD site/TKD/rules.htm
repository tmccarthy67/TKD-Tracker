Rules of the Do Jang

  • Bow upon entering and leaving the class.
  • Use the time before class for stretching and practicing.
  • No student may teach any technique without specific permission from the head instructor.
  • No chewing gum or jewelry may be worn during class.
  • No horseplay is allowed.
  • Sparring is strictly forbidden without specific permission.
  • Colored belt students must bring the minimum sparring gear to every class.
  • Minimum personal sparring gear is mouth guards and head gear for everyone, and groin cups for men and boys.
  • No sparring without minimum personal sparring gear is allowed.
