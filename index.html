Tae Kwon Do

Tae Kwon Do is a Korean martial art, developed by General Choi. Tae Kwon Do (TKD) is loosely translated as "the way of the foot and fist". It was a blend of many martial arts styles, old and new.

Amoung the many benifits of practicing TKD, it developes coordination, balence and relaxation. It can also be a very effective form of self-defence.
