Breaking

3 Principles needed for breaking

  • Speed - More important than how much strength you put behind a punch or kick is how fast your hand or foot moves.
    Force = Mass X Accelleration
    So the faster you move your hand or foot, the more force is transfered to the board.

  • Accuracy - You can't break what you don't hit.
    The farther from the center of the board you hit the more force it takes to break it.

  • Penatration - You need to go through the board to break it.
    If your hand or foot doesn't pass through the board, it will not break.
